Silkie chickens :
the cuddly teddy bears of the poultry world.

If you're looking for a friendly, chatty, sweet-natured breed as a first chicken for your children or a companion for yourself, you may just have found your ideal. Silkie chickens are exactly as they look - funny, cuddly and very kind.

Silkies are gentle, kind birds who love company and adore being held. They tend to chatter away as they follow you around, which they will do once they're about six months old. They make wonderful mothers, in fact it sometimes seems their sole purpose in life is to be a mum!   Read more
